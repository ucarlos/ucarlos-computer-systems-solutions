What would be printed as a result of the following call to show_bytes?
     const char *m = "mnopqr";
     show_bytes((byte_pointer) m, strlen(m));
     
Note that letters ‘a’ through ‘z’ have ASCII codes 0x61 through 0x7A.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Solution:
	m  n  o  p  q  r
	6d 6e 6f 70 71 72
